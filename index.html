About Us.

"Discover the art of karate with Action Sport International School of karate -Action Sport Karate is one of India's top schools for martial arts. Headquartered in Tirur, Malappuram, our commitment to excellence and passion for teaching is unparalleled. Contact us to start your Karate journey today"

At Action Sport Karate International, our approach to teaching karate is holistic, and we aim to provide our students with a complete martial arts education. We believe that the study of karate is not just about learning self-defense techniques, but also about developing the whole person. Our classes are designed to help students develop self-confidence, respect, discipline, and mental fortitude
